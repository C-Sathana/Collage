Research

The IT Department is at the forefront of research in various fields of technology. Our research areas include software engineering, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and more. We collaborate with industry partners and academia to drive innovation and develop cutting-edge solutions.

Ongoing Projects

AI-driven Chatbot Development

Developing an intelligent chatbot to enhance customer interaction and support.

Blockchain Integration in Supply Chain

Exploring blockchain technology to improve transparency and efficiency in supply chain operations.

Augmented Reality for Retail

Developing AR applications to enhance customer experience in retail environments.

Future Foreseen: ML Unleashed!

Implementing machine learning models to forecast trends and improve decision-making.

Creating IoT systems to enhance urban infrastructure and services in smart cities.
